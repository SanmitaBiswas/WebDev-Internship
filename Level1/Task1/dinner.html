Creamy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (any type)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add milk.
  3. Add cooked pasta and stir gently.
  4. Add cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix until creamy.
  5. Serve hot!
